# Glossary

Short definitions for code names and governance terms used in the app and planning docs.

## Code Names

- Cerebro: Governada's spatial governance globe and constellation experience for discovering entities, alignment, factions, and live governance activity.
- Seneca: The AI governance companion that answers questions, guides matching, explains entities, and can choreograph the globe.
- Studio: Focused workspace mode for proposal authoring, proposal review, decision flow, and high-attention governance work.
- Hub: The persona-adaptive home surface at `/`, prioritizing what needs attention now.
- Cockpit: Operator-grade workspace summary, especially DRep/SPO score, risk, sentiment, and action context.

## Governance Terms

- DRep: Delegated Representative; an on-chain voter who represents ADA holders in Cardano governance.
- SPO: Stake Pool Operator; a pool operator whose governance participation affects delegated citizens and some governance actions.
- CC: Constitutional Committee; the body that evaluates whether governance actions comply with Cardano's constitution.
- GHI: Governance Health Index; Governada's composite signal for the overall health of Cardano governance.
- Alignment: A comparison of values, positions, voting behavior, or proposal meaning across people and entities.
- Matching: The recommendation layer that uses alignment and preferences to connect users with representatives, pools, or positions.
- Delegation: A citizen's on-chain selection of a DRep or stake pool to represent governance interests.
- Coverage: How much of the governance action surface a user's DRep and pool representation actually covers.
- Rationale: A human-readable explanation attached to a vote or position.
- CIP: Cardano Improvement Proposal; a standards or process proposal in the Cardano ecosystem.
- CIP-95: Wallet/governance interaction standard used for vote-related flows.
- CIP-100: Metadata standard used for governance rationales and statements.
- CIP-108: Proposal metadata format used by proposal authoring and review flows.
- Treasury: The Cardano community fund and the governance actions that allocate or manage it.
- Epoch: A Cardano time period used for governance, rewards, snapshots, and recurring summaries.
- Snapshot: Stored historical state for scores, alignment, GHI, decentralization, or entity behavior.
